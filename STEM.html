About STEM

STEM stand for the fields of science, technology, engineering and math.

She STEMs

She STEMs is a 10-week program for girls in the East Bay Area teaching about the basics of electrical engineering, circuits to pitching, and entrepreneurship by working with hardware development and learning about the different types of engineering.

Society of Women Engineers Scholarships

SWE Scholarships support women pursuing ABET-accredited bachelor or graduate student programs in preparation for careers in engineering, engineering technology and computer science in the United States.

NASA Internships

NASA Internships are competitive awards to support educational opportunities by providing research and operational experiences by integrating interns with career professionals emphasizing mentor-directed, degree-related tasks,while contributing to the advancement of NASA.

iD Tech

iD Tech offers 5 unique summer programs for ages 7-19 paving the way in STEM education, introducing the most relevant, in-demand curriculum to set students on their long-term skill development pathways.

Smith Summer Science and Engineering Program

The Smith Summer Science and Engineering Program (SSEP) is a four-week residential program for exceptional young women with strong interests in science, engineering and medicine.
